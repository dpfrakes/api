If you're in school and, like me, enjoy playing the "what-if" game with your grades, you might find this fun and/or helpful. The faded green range shows your minimum and maximum course grade for finalized assignment/test grades. The light green range shows your "hypothetical" grade range, based on grades you enter as "not final." Eventually I plan to make this into mobile app, but for now, Google Sheets works pretty well. To make your own editable version:
  1. Make sure you're signed in to your Google account
  2. Go here
  3. Click Make a copy
  4. Rename your personal copy
  5. Follow instructions in Columns O/P
